### Interview Preparation
From [[Future Ready Designer 2026]] — you need to interview as a strategic designer, not just a visual one.

**Story Bank** — prepare 5-7 stories using STAR format:
1. A time you reframed a problem (pricing page?)
2. A time you influenced stakeholders
3. A time you used data to drive design decisions
4. A time you failed and what you learned
5. A time you led without authority
6. A portfolio walkthrough (7-minute version)

- [ ] Write out story bank
- [ ] Practice portfolio walkthrough (record yourself, time it)
- [ ] Do 2 mock interviews with friends or mentors before real ones

### Automation Ideas

- Use AI to research companies before interviews

- Auto-track applications with a simple sheet or Notion/Obsidian tracker
