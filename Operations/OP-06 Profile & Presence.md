## Why This Matters

From [[2026 Strategy]]: *"Full tilt socialisation and social media overhaul."* Your online presence is your first impression at scale. A strong profile doesn't replace going out — it multiplies it. People will look you up after meeting you. Dating apps filter on photos and bio. Shaadi profiles get filtered by family. All of this needs to be tight before you start putting yourself out there.

## Phase 1: Photo Sprint (Day 1)

You need 5-7 strong photos. Not selfies. Not group shots where people have to guess which one is you.

**What you need:**
1. **Hero shot** — solo, outdoors, natural light, genuine smile, good outfit
2. **Activity shot** — martial arts class, cooking, reading, hiking from USA trip
3. **Social shot** — with friends, laughing, clearly having a good time
4. **Style shot** — well-dressed, intentional outfit, clean background
5. **Travel shot** — pick the best from SF/Vegas/Grand Canyon trip
6. **Candid** — someone caught you in the middle of something real

**How to get them:**
- Ask a friend to take intentional photos this weekend (not posed, but directed)
- Use a tripod + timer on your phone in good light (golden hour on the terrace)
- Review USA trip photos for any strong candids
- Dress well for these (from wardrobe overhaul — even just your best current outfit)
