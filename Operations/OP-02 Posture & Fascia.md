## Daily: Morning Posture Routine (12 min)

Pulled directly from [[Posture Correction Routine]]. Do this on the floor, never the bed.

**Release (5 min):**
1. Hip Flexor Couch Stretch — 90 sec each side
2. Chest Doorway Stretch — 60 sec each side (high + low)
3. Cat-Cow — 10 slow reps

**Activate (7 min):**
1. Dead Bugs — 3 x 10 each side (key move — lower back pressed into floor)
2. Glute Bridges with Pelvic Tilt — 3 x 15 (2 sec hold at top)
3. Wall Slides — 3 x 10 (full back contact)

## Throughout the Day: Stack Checks

Every 30 minutes at desk:
- Ribs down
- Tailbone slightly tucked
- Ears over shoulders
- No forward head

Set a 30-minute timer if needed. This is where the real change happens — the exercises build capacity, the awareness creates lasting change.

## New: Myofascial Release Protocol (3x/week — Tue, Thu, Sun)

This is the missing piece you flagged. Add this after your workout or during evening wind-down.

**Equipment needed:** Foam roller + lacrosse ball (or tennis ball)

**Routine (15-20 min):**

1. **Foam Roll Thoracic Spine** — 2 min
   - Lie on roller at mid-back. Arms crossed over chest. Roll slowly up and down, pausing on tight spots.

2. **Foam Roll IT Band & Quads** — 2 min each side
   - Side-lying for IT band. Face-down for quads. Go slow.

3. **Lacrosse Ball Glute Release** — 90 sec each side
   - Sit on ball, cross one ankle over opposite knee. Roll around until you find the knot. Stay on it.

4. **Lacrosse Ball Upper Trap / Neck** — 90 sec each side
   - Stand against wall, ball between wall and upper trap. Roll gently.

5. **Plantar Fascia Roll** — 60 sec each foot
   - Stand, roll ball under foot arch. Especially important if you're on your feet a lot.

6. **Diaphragm Breathing** — 2 min
   - Lie on back, knees bent. Breathe into belly for 4 counts, hold 4, exhale 6. This releases the deep front line fascia.
