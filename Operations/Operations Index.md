## Sequencing

These domains aren't sequential — they run in parallel. But they have a priority order for the first month:

**Week 1 (March 12–18): Body first**
- Grocery run, kitchen stocked, posture routine restarted, sunlight
- Book medical appointments
- Financial audit started

**Week 2 (March 19–25): Add social**
- Dating profiles revamped
- First event or meetup identified
- Portfolio case study #1 started

**Week 3 (March 26–April 1): Career engine on**
- Portfolio work in motion
- First networking outreach sent
- BrowserStack course done
- All three domains running simultaneously

**April onward: Maintain and push**
- Weekly rhythm locked across all domains
- Sunday review covers all three
- Adjust operations based on what's actually working

## The Weekly Review (Sunday)

During your Sunday "Reload" from [[Perfect Week Index]], answer these:

### Body
- Did I cook at home most days?
- Am I doing posture work daily?
- Did I get sunlight?
- How's my sleep?

### Social
- How many new people did I meet?
- Did I go on a date or attend an event?
- Am I following up with contacts?

### Career
- Did I work on portfolio this week?
- Did I send outreach messages?
- How's the financial runway?

### Meta
- Which domain got neglected?
- What's the one thing that would make the biggest difference next week?
- Am I being too hard on myself or too easy?
