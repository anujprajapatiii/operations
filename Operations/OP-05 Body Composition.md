# OP-05: Body Composition

> **Parent:** [[Body & Health Overhaul]]
> **Status:** Not started
> **Goal:** Workout schedule calibrated to maintenance mode, consistent 5x/week movement

---

## Context

From [[2026 Strategy]]: *"Scale it down a notch into maintenance mode. It's a bit too taxing right now. Let's keep the consistency going but maintaining it will be difficult at its full force."*

The goal isn't to go hard — it's to keep the engine running while you're managing job hunting, social life, and everything else. Consistency over intensity.

---

- 10 min bodyweight warm-up: dead bugs, glute bridges, wall slides
- Kegels: 2 min at end of session

---

## Maintenance Mode Principles

1. **3 resistance days, not 5.** Mon/Wed/Fri. Full body or upper/lower split.
2. **Keep the martial arts Saturday.** This is the non-negotiable — it's social, physical, and fun.
3. **Zone 2 is walking.** Don't overthink it. A 30-min brisk walk counts.
4. **Sunday is recovery, not rest.** Move, but don't train. Long walk, swim, foam roll.
5. **Track the streak, not the performance.** Did you move today? Yes/no. That's the metric.

---

## Resistance Training Framework (Mon/Wed/Fri)

Keep it simple. Pick 4-5 compound movements per session:

**Option A — Push Focus:**
- Push-ups or bench press: 3 x 10-12
- Overhead press: 3 x 10
- Rows: 3 x 12
- Squats or lunges: 3 x 12
- Core: Dead bugs or planks: 2 x 30 sec

**Option B — Pull Focus:**
- Pull-ups or lat pulldowns: 3 x 8-10
- Deadlifts or hip hinges: 3 x 10
- Dumbbell bench: 3 x 12
- Step-ups: 3 x 10 each side
- Core: Pallof press or ab wheel: 2 x 10

Alternate A and B through the week. Don't overthink programming — consistency matters more than optimisation right now.

## The Minimum Viable Day

If the schedule falls apart, the absolute minimum is:
- 20-minute walk outside
- 10 minutes of posture work (from [[OP-02 Posture & Fascia]])

That's it. Don't let a busy day become a zero day.
