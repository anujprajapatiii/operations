# OP-01: Nutrition Reboot

> **Parent:** [[Body & Health Overhaul]]
> **Status:** Not started
> **Goal:** Kitchen stocked, meals cooked, supplements flowing — within 3 days

---

## The Problem

You just got back from travel. Fridge is empty, routine is broken, Swiggy is tempting. Your own protocol says it: *"If you're ordering Swiggy, something has already gone wrong."*

---

## Phase 1: Stock the Kitchen (Day 1)

### Grocery Run — Essential List

**Proteins:**
- Eggs (2 dozen)
- Chicken breast or thighs (1 kg)
- Fish — salmon or sardines (500g)
- Lentils — red and black (500g each)
- Chickpeas — canned or dried (2 cans / 500g)

**Vegetables:**
- Broccoli (500g)
- Cauliflower (1 head + 1 bag cauliflower rice)
- Spinach (1 bunch)
- Carrots (500g)
- Onions (1 kg)
- Garlic (1 head)
- Ginger (1 piece)
- Bell peppers — red and green (4)
- Sweet potatoes (500g)
- Mushrooms — cremini or shiitake (200g)

**Fruits:**
- Berries — any mix (blueberry, strawberry)
- Bananas (6)
- Avocado (2-3)
- Lemon (4)

**Pantry:**
- Rolled oats / steel-cut oats
- Olive oil (extra virgin)
- Coconut oil
- Chia seeds
- Flax seeds
- Hemp seeds
- Walnuts
- Almonds
- Turmeric powder
- Cumin
- Black pepper
- Cinnamon
- Tamari (gluten-free, low sodium)

**Drinks:**
- Green tea
- Chamomile tea
- Matcha powder (if you're out)
- Cacao powder

**Supplements (restock if needed):**
- Omega-3s (high EPA)
- Magnesium
- Vitamin D + K2
- Turmeric + Black Pepper capsules
- Creatine
- Collagen peptides
- L-tyrosine

## Phase 2: First Cook (Day 1-2)

Pick **2 recipes** from [[Blueprint food]] for the week. Recommended starters:

1. **Lemon Red Lentil Soup** — easy, batch-friendly, gut-friendly
2. **Vegetable Stir Fry with Cauliflower Rice** — quick weeknight meal

Cook enough for 3-4 servings each. Store in glass containers.

## Phase 3: Daily Rhythm (Ongoing)

From [[THE PERFECT DAY]] and [[Food Guide]]:

| Meal | Time | What |
|------|------|------|
| Super Water | 7:00 AM | 500ml water + chia seeds + creatine |
| Super Coffee | 7:40 AM | Black coffee or matcha + cacao |
| Supplements | 7:40 AM | Omega-3, Mag, D+K2, Turmeric |
| Breakfast | 8:30 AM | Oats + berries + yoghurt + eggs |
| Lunch | 12:30 PM | Batch-cooked meal from recipe rotation |
| Dinner | 7:00 PM | Protein + roasted veg (kitchen closes 7:30) |

**Rules from your own notes:**
- Eating window: after breakfast through 7 PM
- Heaviest meal before 7 PM cutoff
- Cheat meals once every 3 weeks max
- Half plate vegetables, quarter protein, quarter healthy fats
- No Swiggy. Ever. Eggs take 5 minutes.
