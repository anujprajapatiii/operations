## The Core Principle

From [[Social note]]: *"Everyone is shy. Other people are waiting for you to introduce yourself to them."*

From [[2026 Strategy]]: *"Weekends need to be occupied. Full tilt socialisation."*

Surface area is the game. You don't need to be charming. You need to be present, consistent, and approachable. The math works in your favour if you just show up enough.

## The Channels

### 1. Events from Apple Calendar
From [[Todo Back Home]] item 1. You already have events bookmarked — the issue is attending them.

- [ ] Review Apple Calendar for upcoming events this month
- [ ] Pick 2 events minimum for the next 2 weeks
- [ ] Block the time. Treat them as non-negotiable as a work meeting.
- [ ] After each event: add 1 person on Instagram or exchange numbers

### 2. Book Club (from [[Book Club Strat]])
Your own research shows book clubs have exceptional gender ratios and natural rapport-building.

- [ ] Search for book clubs in Powai / South Mumbai
- [ ] Check Meetup.com, Facebook groups, Instagram local pages
- [ ] Join one. Attend the first session within 2 weeks.
- [ ] Strategy: be genuinely engaged with the book, be social with everyone, don't target anyone. Let connections form naturally.

### 3. Martial Arts / Group Fitness (Saturday)
From [[THE PERFECT DAY]] — Saturday is Krav Maga / Boxing day. This is social by default.

- [ ] Find a class in Powai if you don't have one
- [ ] Commit to every Saturday
- [ ] Talk to people before and after class — it's the easiest icebreaker

### 4. Coffee Circuit (from Todo Back Home)
"Girl dinner coffee home circuit" — this is about creating low-pressure social situations at home or at local spots.

- [ ] Identify 3 coffee shops / cafes in Powai that feel good to hang out at
- [ ] Make one of them your "regular" spot — go 2-3 times per week
- [ ] Invite someone for coffee once a week (friend, colleague, match, anyone)
- [ ] Host at home occasionally — casual dinner or chai with friends

### 5. Meetups & Events
From [[Todo Back Home]]: *"Have a PLAN for the Meet-Up."*

**Before any meetup:**
- [ ] Know what the event is about
- [ ] Have 2-3 genuine questions ready (about the topic, not pickup lines)
- [ ] Dress well (reference wardrobe overhaul)
- [ ] Set a goal: talk to 3 new people minimum
- [ ] Follow up with 1 person within 24 hours (Instagram DM, WhatsApp)

**Where to find events:**
- Meetup.com
- Insider.in
- Instagram local event pages
- Facebook community groups
- Apple Calendar (events you've already saved)
- BrowserStack or work community events

## The Anti-Bender Protocol

From your journal, the pattern is clear: *Saturday dopamine binge → Sunday rehabilitation → Monday cycle repeats.* This is the enemy of social momentum.

**Rules:**
1. Saturday night is for going out, not staying in and bingeing
2. If you don't have plans by Friday evening, make them — even if it's just going to your regular coffee spot
3. No gaming on Saturday. Zero tolerance. That time is for people.
4. Sunday morning: call someone. This anchors the weekend in connection, not recovery.
